About Me

FŠ

JUDr. František Štourač

Attorney, Member of the Czech Bar Association

I am a graduate of the Faculty of Law at the University of West Bohemia in Pilsen. I completed my rigorous examinations at the Faculty of Law at Masaryk University in Brno. I am registered with the Czech Bar Association under registration number 14500.

For over two decades, I have been providing legal services to both individual and corporate clients. I specialize primarily in criminal law, corporate law, family law, and real estate law.

I cooperate with Štourač Law Office, which is located in the prestigious premises of AZ Tower in Brno.
